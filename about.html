Design is complicated. When I walk through the city and stare at 1000ft buildings built from steel, concrete and glass – the main consideration I have is – how the hell did anyone build that? Why does that overhanging beam hang there? Who put that there? Who thought of it? And why didn't they think of doing something different that day?

Design maxims however are simple. They are short statements on what design is, what it can, and can't be. I find designers to be among the most interesting people. I take great inspiration from my peers in the design industry and I'm constantly being influenced by their work and ideas. This is a place where I share those ideas with everyone who wishes to visit.
